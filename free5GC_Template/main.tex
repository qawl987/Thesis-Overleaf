\section*{Ethics and Privacy Statement}
The experiments use emulated user equipment and synthetic traffic only. No human-subject data, production subscriber information, or real operator traffic is collected. The proposed automation can improve operational consistency, but incorrect intent policies could also misallocate network resources; deployments should therefore include admission control, audit logs, and operator approval for high-impact changes.
